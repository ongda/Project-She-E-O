About Us

AiTeam is a diverse group consisting of six strong women in the engineering and business field. The group originated from their in-common workplace SAITS before gathering together with their variety of different major backgrounds to build forth on their idea of the LocGo app. The LocGo app was invented due to the first hand experience from the AiTeam group themselves finding the convenience need of having an app guiding anyone lost to their seek destination and showing the available utilities in every destination.
